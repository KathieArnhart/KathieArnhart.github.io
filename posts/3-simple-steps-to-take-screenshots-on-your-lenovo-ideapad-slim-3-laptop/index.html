On the Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 3 laptop, taking a screenshot is easy with the PrtScn button and the Screen Snipping Tool. Here are the steps to take a screenshot on your Lenovo laptop:

Finally, open an image editor such as Paint and paste the screenshot to save it. You can also press Ctrl + V to paste the screenshot and save it. Now you’re all set to capture and share your screenshots easily on your Lenovo IdeaPad Slim 3 laptop.
