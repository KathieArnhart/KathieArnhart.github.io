Q1: What is a screenshot? A1: A screenshot is an image taken of whatever is currently being displayed on a computer monitor, television, or other visual output device.

Q2: Why would someone want to take a screenshot on their Lenovo laptop running Windows 7? A2: Taking a screenshot is an easy way to capture and share whatever is currently being displayed on your device. It can be helpful when troubleshooting technical issues or trying to demonstrate a specific feature.

Q3: How do you take a screenshot on a Lenovo laptop running Windows 7? A3: On a Windows 7 PC, press the Print Screen key (or “PrtScn”) to take a screenshot of the entire desktop. To take a screenshot of the active window only, press Alt + Print Screen. Then, open the Paint app and paste the screenshot.

Q4: How do you save the screenshot after taking it? A4: After pasting the screenshot in Paint, go to File –> Save As and choose the location and file type you want to save it as (such as a JPEG or PNG).
