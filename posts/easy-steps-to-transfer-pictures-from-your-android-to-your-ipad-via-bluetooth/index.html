1. Activate the Bluetooth Feature#

Activating Bluetooth is the first step to transfer pictures from your Android to your iPad. To begin, go to your Android phone’s Settings and select the Network & Internet option. On this page, you will find the Bluetooth icon. Toggle this to enable Bluetooth on your Android phone. After that, ensure that you also enable Bluetooth on your iPad. To do this, go to Settings and select the Bluetooth option. Once both devices are enabled, you will see them listed in each devices’ Bluetooth settings.

After that, click on your Android phone’s name, found in the Bluetooth settings on your iPad. This will create a connection between your devices. Once the connection is established, you will be prompted to enter a code that is shown on both devices. When you’ve entered the code correctly, the two devices will be connected.

From your Android phone, open the gallery and select the pictures you want to transfer. Click the share icon and select the wireless transfer option. In the list of options, you will see your iPad’s name. Select this, and the pictures will begin transferring. You will receive a notification when the transfer is complete.

Finally, go to the Photos library on your iPad to view the pictures from your Android. Congratulations, now you know how to transfer pictures from your Android to your iPad via Bluetooth.

Step-by-Step Guide to Activate Bluetooth Feature#

First of all, you need to activate the Bluetooth feature on both your Android device and your iPad. You can do this by going to your device settings and turning the Bluetooth switch to ‘on’. Once the Bluetooth is activated on both devices, make sure the devices are close to each other and that the Bluetooth settings of both devices can detect each other.

On your Android device, select the photos you want to transfer and tap the “Share” option. Then select the “Bluetooth” option from the list of sharing options. This will start the scanning process and your iPad will appear as a device in the Bluetooth list.

Choose the iPad and tap on it to connect. Once the connection is established, select the “Send” option and the photos will start transferring to the iPad. Once the transfer is complete, a notification will appear on both devices informing you of the successful transfer.

Finally, on the iPad, you can find the photos by going to Photos app and selecting the “Albums”. All transferred pictures should appear in a separate album. This is how you can transfer pictures from Android to iPad via Bluetooth.
