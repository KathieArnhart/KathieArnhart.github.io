Taking screenshots of a specific area or a portion of your screen is one of the essential tasks of any computer user. Fortunately, it’s easy to take a screenshot of a selected area in Windows. This article guides you through the steps needed to take a screenshot of a selected area in Windows.

First, press the “Print Screen” key on your keyboard. This will copy the image of your current screen to the clipboard. After copying the image to the clipboard, open the Microsoft Paint application by pressing the “Windows” key on your keyboard and typing “Paint” in the search bar.

Once the Paint application is open, press “Ctrl + V” on your keyboard to paste the screenshot from your clipboard. You can now crop the screenshot to only include the portion you want by selecting the “Select” button and using the mouse to draw a rectangle around the area you wish to capture.

When you have finished drawing the selection, press the “Crop” button to only include the selected area in the image. Finally, press the “Save” button to save the screenshot to your computer.
