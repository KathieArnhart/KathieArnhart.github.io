Are you curious to find out how to take a screenshot on your Samsung A70 with the latest Android 11? Read on to learn the 7 easy steps to take a screenshot in no time! Do you know how to make cool screenshots on your Samsung A70? Well, if you don’t know where to start, here’s a guide on how to take a screenshot on Samsung A70 with Android 11. It’s actually pretty easy—you just need to follow these seven simple steps!

First, press and hold the power button and the volume down button. You’ll know you’ve done it right if you feel a short vibration. This will capture the current screen, and the screenshot will be added to the notification tray.

Second, open the Notification Shade and you’ll find the screenshot. You can tap on the screenshot to quickly edit or share it.

Third, if you want to save the screenshot without the hassle of editing or sharing, you can press the Recent apps button. This will open up a list of all the screenshots you’ve taken. From here, you can tap on the screenshot and select the option to save it.

Fourth, if you want to save or share the screenshot in a different way, you can open the Gallery app. Here you can access and view the screenshot, and you can also choose to edit or share the screenshot.

Fifth, if you want to take a screenshot of another app, you can press and hold the power button and the volume down button for a second time. You’ll again feel a short vibration, and the screenshot will be added to the Notification Shade.

Sixth, if you want to capture a specific area of your screen, you can press and hold the power button and the volume down button for a third time. You’ll feel a longer vibration, and this will activate a rectangular selection tool. You can then drag your finger over the part of the screen you want to capture. When you’re finished, just release your finger and the screenshot will be added to the notification tray.

Finally, if you want to access all the screenshots you’ve taken, you can open up the Gallery app and look in the Screenshots folder. Here you can view, edit, and share all the screenshots you’ve taken.

There you have it! Seven easy steps to take a screenshot on Samsung A70 with Android 11. So the next time you want to take a screenshot, you won’t have to worry—you’ll know exactly what to do.
