- How to Clear Cache On TCL Android TV#

Cleaning up the memory on your TCL Android TV can be fast and easy with a few simple steps. Clearing the application cache, which stores temporary files and helps apps load faster, is a great way to free up space on your TV. This guide will walk you through how to do it.

First, go to the ‘Settings’ menu of your TV and scroll down to the ‘System’ tab. Click on this tab, then scroll down to the ‘Storage & Reset’ option. Under this option, you will find the ‘Clear Application Data’ button. Click on this.

This will bring up a list of all the applications that are installed on your TV. Select the app(s) you would like to clear the cache for and click ‘Clear Data’. The process might take a few minutes, so be patient.

Once the process is complete, you can go back to the ‘Storage & Reset’ option to check how much space you have freed up. You may also want to check the performance of your apps to see if they are now running faster and smoother.
