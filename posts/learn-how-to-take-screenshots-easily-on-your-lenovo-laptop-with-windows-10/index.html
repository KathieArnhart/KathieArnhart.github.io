Q : What is a screenshot?

A : A screenshot is an image capture of the contents displayed on the screen of a computer, laptop, smartphone, or other electronic devices.

Q : How do I take a screenshot on my Lenovo Laptop with Windows 10?

A : Taking a screenshot on a Lenovo laptop with Windows 10 is simple. All you need to do is press the Windows key and PrtScn (Print Screen) at the same time. Your screenshot will be saved in the “Pictures” folder in your laptop.

Q : What do I do if I want to take a screenshot of the active window?

A : If you only want to take a screenshot of the active window, press the Alt and PrtScn keys together. This will capture the active window and save it in the same “Pictures” folder.

Q : What if I want to take a screenshot of a specific area of my laptop?

Q : How do I access the screenshots I’ve taken?

A : To access the screenshots you have taken, open the “Pictures” folder on your laptop. It will be located in the “This PC” directory and all of your screenshots will be stored in this folder.
