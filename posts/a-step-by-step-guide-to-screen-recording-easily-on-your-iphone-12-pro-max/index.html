Second, you can also add a commentary or voice-over when you are recording. To do this, tap the microphone icon before you start recording. This way, you can make voice comments, narrations, or instructions while the recording is running.

Third, if you want to end the recording, you can either tap the red status bar at the top of your screen or press the home button twice and then swipe right or left to close the Control Center. You will be notified once the recording has been saved.

Finally, you can always check your recordings by going to the Photos app and selecting video album. Here, you can see, play, and delete any recordings you want. With these steps, you can now easily screen record on your iPhone 12 pro max.

Making a screen recording of your iPhone 12 Pro Max is easier than you think. All it takes is a few taps to record the footage you need. The process is straightforward and can be done in just a few simple steps. Here is how to screen record on your iPhone 12 Pro Max:

Next, you can choose whether you want to capture sound from your microphone or from the entire audio playing on your phone.
