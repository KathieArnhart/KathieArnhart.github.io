Q1. What are the easy steps for taking a screenshot on Microsoft Surface Laptop Go? A1. Taking a screenshot on Microsoft Surface Laptop Go is actually very easy and simple. There are 4 easy steps to follow:

  1. Press and hold the “Power” and “Volume down” buttons together for 2 seconds
  2. The display will briefly dim, and the screenshot should be saved to your Pictures folder
  3. To locate and open the screenshot, go to Start > File Explorer > Pictures
  4. You can also open the screenshot from the Notification Area in the bottom-right corner of your screen

Q2. What should I do if the screenshot doesn’t appear in the Pictures folder? A2. If the screenshot doesn’t appear in the Pictures folder, there might be a few reasons for it:

  1. The screenshot may not be saved in the Pictures folder at all.
  2. You may have saved the screenshot elsewhere.
  3. There might be an error which caused the image not to be saved correctly.

If you are still unable to locate the screenshot, you can always try to take another screenshot to make sure the setting was correct.
