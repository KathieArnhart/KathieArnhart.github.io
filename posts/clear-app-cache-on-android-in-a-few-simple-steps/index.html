- How To Clear App Cache#

Clearing App Cache on Android is one of the best ways to get your phone back into tip top condition. This guide will show you the simple steps to clear your app cache on any Android device.

First, open your Settings app and go to Applications. Here you can see all the apps installed on your phone. Scroll through the list and find the app you want to clear the cache for. Tap on the app and then on Storage. Now tap on either the Clear Cache or Clear Data option.

You can also clear all of your apps’ caches at once. Go back to your Settings and choose Storage. Tap on Cached Data and then select Ok when prompted to clear all cached data.

It’s important to note that if you clear the data, it will delete any preferences you’ve previously set for that app and you’ll also delete user data such as high scores. To be sure, it’s always best to back up your data before clearing the cache.

Another way to clear your apps’ cache is using an app like Clean Master. This app will let you easily clear all of your apps’ cache at once. You can also use Clean Master to delete unwanted or temporary files taking up space on your device, as well as clean up junk files and boost your phone’s performance.
