Second, users need to connect their AirPods to their smartphones. Just open the AirPods case and tap the button on the back of the case. Then, press and hold the AirPods in the case until the light starts flashing and a connection is established.

Third, users need to view their AirPods battery level from the Android settings. Once both AirPods and the case are connected and paired, Android users can view their battery level in the Battery settings.

Finally, users can also check their AirPods battery level from the Bluetooth settings. Go to Settings > Connected Devices > Bluetooth and look for the AirPods in the list. The battery level of the AirPods and the case will show up in percentage next to the AirPods.
