2. Methods to take screenshots in Steam VR#

Taking screenshots is easy and fun when playing a game in Steam VR. You can use these screenshots to share with other players or simply for personal collection. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to take screenshots in Steam VR.

First, open the Steam VR Dashboard by pressing the System button on your controllers. Next, click the menu button on the top right corner of the dashboard. This will open up a menu with multiple options. Select the “Screenshot” option to access the screenshot settings.

You can now use the A and X buttons on your controllers to change the screenshot settings. You can choose to take a screenshot in VR or a panoramic screenshot. Make sure to also adjust the picture size and quality to ensure that you get the best quality screenshot.

When you are done with the settings, click the “Take Screenshot” button on the center of the screenshot settings page. Your screenshot will now be saved to your computer. You can access the screenshots by going to Steam Library and selecting the “Screenshots” tab.

Lastly, you can share your screenshots with other players by going to the same Screenshots tab and selecting the “Share” option. You can now select the platform to upload your screenshot or directly share the link. Enjoy sharing your screenshots with your friends.
