A. How to Take Screenshot on Windows 10 Lenovo Laptop#

Taking screenshots on Windows 10 Lenovo laptops can be done easily, with no additional software required. To capture the screen image, simply press the PrtScn (Print Screen) key on your keyboard. The PrtScn key will take a snapshot of the entire laptop screen. This image will be stored on the clipboard.

The second way of taking screenshots on your Windows 10 Lenovo laptop is by pressing the Windows + PrtSCn key combination. This will take a screenshot of the whole screen and save it automatically as a .png file in the Pictures > Screenshots folder.

In addition to the traditional methods of taking screenshots, Windows 10 offers a Snipping Tool. This tool allows users to capture a portion of the laptop screen and save it as an image file. To open the Snipping Tool, just type “Snipping Tool” in the Windows search bar.

Another way to take screenshots is to use the Game Bar. To access the Game Bar, press the Windows + G key combination on your keyboard. From here, you can select the screenshot icon located at the top right of the Game Bar interface. This will capture the whole laptop screen and save it to your Pictures folder.

With these methods, taking screenshots on Windows 10 Lenovo laptops is a simple process. Follow these steps to easily capture screen images and save them!
