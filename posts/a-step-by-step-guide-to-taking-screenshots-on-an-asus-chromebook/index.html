1. What is a Chromebook?#

A Chromebook is a laptop or tablet running the Chrome OS operating system. ASUS Chromebooks come with a range of features that enhance their usability and capabilities. One of these features is the ability to take screenshots, which can be incredibly helpful for capturing and sharing images or displaying technical instructions. This guide will take you through the steps needed for taking screenshots on an ASUS Chromebook.

First you need to decide which type of screenshot you would like to take – a full window screenshot or a custom region. To take a full window screenshot simply press the combination of keys Ctrl+Window Switch. To take a custom area screenshot you’ll need to press Ctrl+Shift+Window Switch and then select the area you want to capture with your mouse.

Once you’ve selected the area you’d like to screenshot you’ll need to press the Enter key on your keyboard to capture the image. The image will be saved to the Downloads folder and you can open it and edit it with any image editing software.

Finally, to share your screenshot you can attach it to an email, post it to social media or simply copy and paste it into an online document. When saving your screenshot, make sure to save it as a .JPEG or .PNG image as these are the most commonly used image file formats.

Using these simple steps anyone can easily take screenshots on their ASUS Chromebook and share them with family, friends and colleagues.
