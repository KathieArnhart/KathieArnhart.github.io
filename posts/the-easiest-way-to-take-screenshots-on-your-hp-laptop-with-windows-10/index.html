Next, press the ‘Alt’ key and the ‘Print Screen’ key at the same time, and the cursor on your screen will change to a cross-hair. Now, click and drag around the area of the screen that you want to take a screenshot of. The screenshot will only capture the area inside the selection box.

Once you’ve released the mouse button, the screenshot will be taken and will appear in the Photos app in the ‘Screenshots’ folder.
