1. Knowledge on Screenshot Basics#

Taking screenshots on a laptop is an essential step to learn for any user. For this tutorial, we will be walking you through the basics of capturing screenshots on a Windows 11 Acer laptop. First, press the “Windows + PrtSc” keys simultaneously to capture the entire screen and save it in the “Screenshots” folder located in the Pictures library. Users can also make a selection of just a portion of the screen by pressing the “Windows + Shift + S” keys at the same time to create a snipping tool. Furthermore, for users who want to capture an active window, press the “Alt + PrtSc” keys to capture a screenshot of the active window; note that this screenshot won’t be saved automatically. Once you’ve taken your screenshot, open the Paint app and press “Ctrl + V” to paste the screenshot in the app. Finally, click the floppy disk icon to save the screenshot with your desired filename and file format. With these steps, you can easily capture screenshots on your Windows 11 Acer laptop.

The third way is to use the Snipping Tool. You can access it by pressing the Windows logo + R buttons, type snippingtool and hit Enter. Once the tool is open, you can select the type of snip you’d like to take and press New. The image will be automatically saved in the Pictures folder.

By following these steps, you can easily capture screenshots on Windows 11 Acer laptop. You can then use them for work, school, or even just for fun.
