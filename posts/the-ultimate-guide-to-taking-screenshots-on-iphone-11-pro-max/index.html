Q.5 How do I delete a screenshot on my iPhone 11 Pro Max? A. You can easily delete a screenshot on your iPhone 11 Pro Max by opening the Photos app and selecting the Screenshots folder. Select the screenshot you want to delete and tap on the Trash icon. The screenshot will then be deleted.
