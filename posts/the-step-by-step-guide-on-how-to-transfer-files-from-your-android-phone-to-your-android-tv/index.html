2. Transfering Files#

Connecting your Android phone to an Android TV can be a great way to share content between your devices. In this tutorial, we’ll be showing you how easy it is to transfer files from your Android phone to your Android TV. Here’s what you need to do:

First, make sure that both your Android phone and TV are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Then, open the ‘Settings’ app on your Android phone and select ‘Display’. Next, select ‘Cast Screen/Audio’ and then select your Android TV from the list of available devices.

Once the connection is established, open the file you would like to send to your TV. Tap the ‘Share’ icon and select your Android TV from the list of share options. When you do this, the content of the file will start playing on your TV.

Finally, if you would like to exit sharing mode, open the ‘Settings’ app again on your Android phone and select ‘Cast Screen/Audio’. Then, select ‘Disconnect’ to end the connection and you will be ready to go.
