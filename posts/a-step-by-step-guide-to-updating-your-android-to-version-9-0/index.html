3. Updating Your Device#

It’s important to regularly update your Android device to the latest OS version not only to benefit from new features, but also for security purposes. Updating your device to the latest version of Android is easy and usually takes less than 10 minutes. Here are the steps to update your Android to version 9.0.

First, open the Settings menu. This can be done by swiping down from the top of the screen and tapping on the gear-shaped icon. From the Settings menu, scroll down and tap on System, then Advanced, and finally tap on System Update.

Second, tap on Check for Update. The device will look for the latest version available and if an update is available, it will start downloading automatically. Depending on the size of the update, this could take anywhere from five to thirty minutes to finish.

Third, after the download is completed, you’ll be asked to install the update. Tap on Install Now and your device will restart to apply the changes. Once the update has finished installing, your device will boot up with the new version of Android.

Finally, enjoy the new OS version! Remember to regularly check for system updates to ensure your device is up to date with the latest security patches and other improvements.
