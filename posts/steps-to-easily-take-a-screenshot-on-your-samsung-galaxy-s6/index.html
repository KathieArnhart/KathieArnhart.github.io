2. Tips and Trick to Easily Take a Screenshot on Your Galaxy S6#

Taking screenshot on your Samsung Galaxy S6 is an essential task. To help you get the best results, here are the tips and tricks to easily take a screenshot on your Samsung Galaxy S6.

The first one is to use the physical buttons on your phone. All you have to do is to press and hold the home and power key for a second and your phone will produce a shutter sound. Then you can find the screenshot in the Gallery under the Screenshots folder.

The second one is to use the motion and gesture option. You can turn it on by going to the motion and gesture settings and turning on palm swipe to capture. After activating this option, you just have to swipe the edge of your hand across the screen to take a screenshot.

The third one is to use S Finder or S Voice. By pressing the S Finder button on the Quick Settings bar, you can enable the voice command and say ‘capture screen’. Then your device will take the screenshot immediately.

The last one is to use the Assistant Menu. To use this option, you have to turn it on by going to the settings and enable assistant menu. Then you can find the screenshot icon in the assistant menu and click it to take a screenshot.
