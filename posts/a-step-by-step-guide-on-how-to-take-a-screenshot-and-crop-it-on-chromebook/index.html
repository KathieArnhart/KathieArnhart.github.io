Have you ever had a moment when you just wanted to take a screenshot of something you saw on your Chromebook? Taking screenshots of important or particularly meaningful pieces of information can be a great way to capture a moment in time. It’s also incredibly easy to do on a Chromebook, as long as you know the right steps. In this article, I’ll be giving you a step-by-step guide on how to capture and crop screenshots on your Chromebook. So, if you’ve ever wanted a easy way to take screenshots on your Chromebook, here’s your chance to learn how!

First, you need to open the window or screen that you want to capture. Once you’ve done this, press the Ctrl and Window Switcher keys at the same time (the Window Switcher is a key with four boxes that’s located next to the Alt key). This will capture a screenshot of the whole window or screen.

Next, you’ll need to crop the screenshot to only include the part of the screen you want. To do this, right-click on the screenshot and select “Crop Image” from the menu that appears. This will open a small window where you can adjust the area of the screenshot you want to keep. When you’re done adjusting the image, click the “Crop” button.

And that’s it! You now have a cropped screenshot of the part of the screen you wanted. Capturing and cropping screenshots on your Chromebook is easy and it’s a great way to save memorable pieces of information. Plus, now that you know the steps, you can take screenshots anytime you want!

1. Overview How to Take a Screenshot on Chromebook#

Taking a screenshot and cropping it on Chromebook is a convenient and quick way to edit certain images. In just a few steps, you’ll be able to save a picture that you can use for any purpose. Here’s how to do it:

First, find the Screenshot button on your Chromebook’s virtual keyboard. It looks like a square with two lines next to it and is usually on the top right of the keyboard. Once you’ve found it, press the button and the screenshot will be taken.

Second, locate the screenshot you have just taken. It’s usually saved in the Downloads folder. You can then open the image in the Chromebook’s image editor.

Third, select the area you want to crop from your screenshot. When you have selected it, click the crop button and the image will be cropped.

Finally, save the cropped screenshot. Now your screenshot is ready to be used.

By following these steps, you can quickly and easily take a screenshot and crop it on your Chromebook.

ii. Cropping a Screenshot on Chromebook#

You can choose to save the cropped image in the same file location. If you want to save the image to a different location, simply select the Save As option and choose the desired saving destination.

To resize the cropped image, select the Image option located on the top bar and then select Image Size. A window will appear where you can enter the desired height and width to resize the image. Lastly, click Save for the image to be resized.

By following these steps, you can easily take a screenshot of your Chromebook and crop it to the size your need.

2. Cropping the Screenshot#

Cropping a screenshot on a Chromebook is simple and quick once you learn the steps. Here are the steps for cropping a screenshot on a Chromebook.

First, take a screenshot of whatever you want to crop. To do this, press the Ctrl + Switch Window key at the same time. This will take a screenshot of everything displayed on the Chromebook’s monitor.

Next, open the cropping tool. To open the cropping tool, press the Ctrl + Shift + Window Switcher button. After the cropping tool appears, click and drag the mouse to create a box around the area of the screenshot that you want to crop.

Once you’ve created the box, click the Crop button in the lower-right corner. This will crop the image to the selected area.

Finally, save the cropped screenshot. To save, press the Ctrl + S keys. This will open a window where you can save the cropped screenshot with a file name and location.

Now that you know how to take a screenshot on a Chromebook and crop it, you can quickly and easily crop screenshots to the size and shape desired.
