Once you open the app, ensure that the microphone is turned on and your sound levels are turned up. Then, tap the red Record button to begin recording your voice message. When you are done, tap the stop button, and then choose to either save the message, or discard it.

If you wish to listen to the message you just recorded, tap the play button. If you decide to keep the message, choose the save option and select a name for the file. The message will be stored in the Voice Recorder folder in your files.
