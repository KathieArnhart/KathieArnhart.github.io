Taking screenshots is very useful for presentations, blog posts or capturing an important moment that you would like to share with others. Today, I’m going to show you a step-by-step guide on how to take screenshots on a Lenovo laptop with Windows 7 Ultimate.

Let’s get started.

Firstly, check to ensure that the Print Screen key is available on your keyboard. If it is, the key should be located right above the number pad – or in some cases, right next to the Delete and Insert keys.

Secondly, to take a screenshot, press “Alt,” “Ctrl” and “Print Screen” keys simultaneously. This will capture a screenshot of your entire display. If you want to take a screenshot of a certain window, press “Alt,” “Ctrl” and “Print Screen” keys and then click the window you want to capture.

Thirdly, open Paint, an image editing program that comes with Windows 7 Ultimate. Click the “Paste” option under the “Edit” menu, and the screenshot will appear. You can make any necessary changes to the screenshot at this point, such as resizing, cropping or editing the colors.

Fourthly, when you’re satisfied with the image, click “Save As” and select the file format you want. You can choose from a wide range of file formats, such as JPG, PNG or GIF. Then give your image a name, select a location and click “save.”

Congratulations! You have successfully taken a screenshot and saved it. Now you can share the image with your friends or use it in a project.
