This article will teach you how to enable and use the screen recording feature on an iPhone 12 Pro Max.

First, open the Settings app on your iPhone 12 Pro Max and select the Control Center tab. Here, you can customize which features appear in the Control Center. Scroll until you find the Screen Recording option and tap to enable it. This will add the screen recording icon to your Control Center.

Next, open the Control Center by swiping down from the top-right corner of your iPhone 12 Pro Max and tap the Screen Recording icon. This will start the screen recording. You can move the recording icon to the top of the screen and tap the microphone icon in order to enable audio recording during your screen capture.

Once you’re finished recording your screen, simply tap the same screen recording button again in the Control Center and select Stop to save the recording. You can then view the recording in the Photos app. Additionally, you can also access your screen recordings by going to Settings -> Control Center -> Screen Recording.
