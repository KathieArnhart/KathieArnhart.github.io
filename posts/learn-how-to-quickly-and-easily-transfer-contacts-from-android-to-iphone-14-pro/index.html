The next step is to open the iPhone 14 Pro. Connect the iPhone to Wi-Fi to start the transfer process. Now, open Settings and select Accounts & Passwords. Once connected, select either Contacts or Address Book to begin the contact transfer.

The last step is to choose which contacts you would like to transfer over to your iPhone. To do this, open the Accounts & Passwords page and select iCloud.
