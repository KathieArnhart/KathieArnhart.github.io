2. Steps to scan a QR code on Androd 10#

Android 10 has a useful feature allowing users to quickly and easily scan QR codes. It is a simple process that only requires a few steps. Here’s a guide that will walk you through the process of scanning a QR code on Android 10:

To begin, open the Camera app on your Android 10 device. Point your camera at the QR code so that the code is visible within the camera frame. Once the QR code is detected, a notification will appear at the top of the screen. Tap on the notification to open it.

Next, the QR code will be scanned and the contents within it will be revealed. You may be asked to confirm the action or download a specific app before the contents of the QR code is revealed. Accept or decline the request as desired.

For added security, you have the option to review the contents of the QR code before you accept or decline the request. You can find the option to review the code in the settings of the Camera app.

Finally, once you have confirmed the contents of the QR code, you can then proceed with whatever action the QR code was meant to provide, such as visiting a website or downloading an app. Congratulations! You now know how to scan QR codes on Android 10.
